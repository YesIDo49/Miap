We don't get tired of Tintamarre !

Read more


In charge of Tintamarre, Gabrielle Beck, an ex architect, designer, who didn't leave anything to chance during her reconversion by training at Lenôtre. At Tintamarre, she shares her love for Lebanese food through her childhood memories and her know-how. For the starter, vegetables jars and Gabrielle who does her mix for the zaatar.

In our plates ?
Hoummous : Mashed cheakpeas with tahina and lemon.
Cheakpeas fatteh : cooked cheakpeas with coriander and garlic topped with greek yogurt and pine nuts.
Croquettes of caramelized halloumi with honey and zaatar, tzatziki et spicy oil.
Frikeh of stewed beef : green weat, stewed beef with honey and soft spicies, seasonal vegetables.
Moudardara and its perfect egg : lentil with rice perfumed with cumin and pepper, red cabbage fondue.
Poached mandarin and rice pudding with citruses.
Chocolate mouhallabieh and caramelized pistachios.

How much ?
Dishes : 14,50€.
Lunch menu : starter + dish or dish + dessert : 16,50€
Kefta sandwich : 7,50€ / Veggie sandwich : 6,50€. (10€ with a starter).

It is excellent so go ahead !

Aji Dulce, Dolce vita incoming !

Read more

It is a food truck and a Venezuelan restaurant which gives pride to the most emblematic speciality of the Venezuelan street food : arepas. White corn patties, often garnished. Here, they are oven baked in a minute and served hot, crispy and garnished to your liking. You can choose between 9 recipes.
Aji Dulce has a food truck that moves and we can track its movements on their website and social medias, and a shop with a few seating places, in the 9th neighborhood in Saint-Georges.

In our plates ?
Tequenos : doughnut filled with Venezuelan cream cheese.
Arepa Pelua : stewed frayed beef in a vegetable sauce and gouda cheese.
Arepa Catira : rosted chicken stewed in a vegetable sauce, amarillo cheese (gouda)
Arepa Queso : Venezuelan cream cheese, fresh avocado, fried plantain banana.
Tostones : green plantain banana chips lightly salted and Venezuelan cream cheese.

How much ?
Arepas : 10,50€
Lunch menu 13,50€ : Arepa + 2 sauces + starter, dessert or home made juice

Spice up your life ! Source: http://parisianavores.paris/aji-dulce-arepa-restaurant-venezuelien-paris-9/

La brigade du tigre, gare aux représailles !

En lire plus

La Brigade du Tigre, c’est le second restaurant d’Adrien Ferrand, à quelques pas du premier Eels, rue du Faubourg Poissonnière. Le pari ? Un voyage aux quatre coins de l’Asie, de la Thaïlande au Sri-Lanka, des incontournables de la street food aux spécialités traditionnelles, opéré par le chef Galien Emery.

Dans nos assiettes ?
3 menus déjeuner entrée-plat-dessert
Rouleau d’automne – miso rouge
Crab Cake – mayo piment
Raviolis grillés de champignons – Tom Kha pommes de terre
Tataki de boeuf de Galice – black beans
Merlu – chou pointu grillé – combawa gingembre
Poitrine de cochon confite et laquée – légumes verts – riz sauté
Mango sticky rice
Cake coco caramélisé – caramel cacahuète

C’est combien ?
Entrée – plat -dessert : 23€ // Entrée – plat ou plat – dessert : 19€

Avec ça on a une faim de loup ! (enfin de tigre ça le fait mieux) Source: http://parisianavores.paris/la-brigade-du-tigre-restaurant-paris-10/
