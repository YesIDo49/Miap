Your account


Why must you have an account ?
Creating an account will allow you to have access to all of our features. Since our website is also a social media, your account will allow you to secure your personal information in order to share your ideas with your friends or more !

How to create an account ?
Creating an account is very easy. CLick on the button, on the top right, and select the button "Sign in".
You can link your account to your email address.


HOW TO DO IT ?
1. Enter your email address, your name, your first name and create a password.
You can't use 2 similar email addresses. Make sure that it is your right email address before confirming !
2. Click on the "Create my account" button.
3. Cheer up, you are now a Miapy !!


How to log out of your account ?

You want to log out from your account ? Nothing easier !


1. Check on the navigation bar.
2. Clcik on the "Log out" button
3. I told you it was easy !!


How to delete your account ?

Oh misery ! Oh sorrow... We have a man doooooooown !
Just kidding, how to delete an account?


1. Open the navigation bar.
2. Click on the "My profile" button.
3. Open the account's settings.
4. Click on the "Delete my account" button.
Your account will be deleted in 30 days (We're sad but that's how it is...)
